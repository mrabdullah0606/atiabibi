Project Information

  • Role: Data Enumerator and Analyst
  • Project Funded by: Rasta & PIDE

Project Overview

Business Problem: We need to create a commission dashboard for directors that calculates commissions for new contracts while ensuring profit margins are maintained. The challenge is to reward performance appropriately without impacting profitability.

Methdology:

KPI Definition:

Identify measurable performance indicators (e.g., service delivery (Fill Rate %), Confirmation Time in 24 Hours, Same day Requests Filled within 1 Hour, Gross Profit Margin %).

Profit Margin Targeting:

Calculate expected profit margins for the new contract after accounting for additional costs. Set a **minimum threshold margin ** that must be achieved for commission eligibility.

Commission Design:

Link director commission to KPI achievement and profit margin attainment. Apply deductions if performance falls below target (penalty mechanism aligns with marginal incentive theory).

Monitoring & Feedback:

Track KPIs and profit margins periodically.

Optimization:

Use a cost-benefit approach to assess trade-offs between additional hires, service quality, and profit maximization.

Recommendation:

The Success: Mention that the GP Margin (11.32%) is excellent, meaning the business is profitable on the work it does complete.

The Solution:

The strategy wasn't to change the pricing, but to fix the Operational Velocity. By focusing on "Confirmation Time" and "Same-day Requests," you are effectively turning the "Total Balance" from a negative value to a positive one.
